Frequently Asked Questions

How do I apply for ITI?

Follow the roadmap: register, upload documents, take exams, attend interviews, and await acceptance.

What exams are required?

You’ll take an IQ test, English test, and track-specific technical test.

Can I change my track?

Yes, before interviews, you can request a track change via your ITI account.
